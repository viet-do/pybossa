Introduction

Your privacy is important to us, the EasyCOVID-19 Project at the Shriver Center, University of Massachusetts Medical School (Shriver Center/us/we).

This Privacy Notice (Notice) tells you how we collect, use, share, and protect information about you and your use of this EasyCOVID-19 website.

What types of information do we collect about you?

We collect information you voluntarily give us, such as your username, email address, and native language(s).

Why do we collect the information?

We use the information we collect to:

  • make this website fun and easy;
  • send you emails; and
  • help us make COVID-19 information easy to understand.

How do we collect the information?

Our website uses:

  • Cookies, small files of text that collect and store information that help us make visiting our website a good experience for you.
  • Web Forms you voluntarily complete.
  • Email to register (sign up) to use our website.

What do we do with the information?

We may use your information to contact you with announcements about the EasyCOVID-19 Project. We will not sell or rent the information to third parties outside of the Shriver Center without your consent.

Who will have access to the information?

We may share your information with a third party if it needs access to troubleshoot a problem.

We may also share the information with government and law enforcement agencies or regulators to:

  1. comply with a legal process, subpoena, order, or other legal or regulatory requirement applicable to us;
  2. enforce our terms of use or other policies; or
  3. pursue available legal remedies or defend against legal claims.

How do I consent?

By using our website and providing your information, you:

  • agree to this Notice;
  • allow us to send you emails; and
  • allow us and our vendors to collect and use the information you provide.

What about personal information of individuals outside of the United States?

The Shriver Center is located within the United States and subject to United States law. Information related to the Easy Covid-19 Project is published within the United States. Information provided to us by users outside the United States will be transferred to the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those of your home country. By using our website or providing us with your information, you acknowledge your information will be transferred to the United States, processed, and/or stored on servers in the United States. Your information will be used as provided in this Notice. All reasonable steps will be taken to protect your privacy in accordance with applicable data-privacy laws.

How do we protect your information?

We use computer safeguards to protect your information. If you do not want to provide us information about you, please use our anonymous website. The only difference is that it is not as fun to use. Examples: you will not appear on our leaderboard or be part of user teams.

How can you stop receiving emails from us?

You can choose not to receive emails from us by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email you receive from us.

How long will we keep your information?

We keep your information for as long as needed to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. We may also keep your information if legally required or to fulfill a legitimate interest.

How will you be notified if our Privacy Notice changes?

The Shriver Center reserves the right to change, to modify, or to otherwise amend this Notice at its sole discretion and at any time as it deems circumstances warrant. If the Notice changes, the Shriver Center will post an updated privacy statement with a modification date. Please check back often for the most current version. Your continued use of our website after any change in the Privacy Notice will constitute your acceptance of such changes.

How can you contact us?

If you have questions about this Privacy Notice, please contact the University of Massachusetts Medical School's Office of Management at privacyandcompliance@umassmed.edu.
